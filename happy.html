Happy WordPress Loops.

Making happy developers make clients happy since 2011.

Happy Loops has a simple, and singular, mission. We want to help you create better loops with WordPress. There's some common confusion that's gone around for a long time, and we're here to help change that. At Happy Loops, we gather great resources, and show you lots of examples of how to create loops with WordPress the right way.
